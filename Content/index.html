Overview

It's hard to attain, focus, yet is lost with a ting sound. Be it the invincible feeling youth or a well-rehearsed experienced driver, it takes a moment to get distracted, leaving behind a trail of sad stories.

Out of 1.25 million deaths and tens of millions of injuries that result from road traffic accidents every year, a significant number of these accidents are caused by distracted drivers, especially drivers using mobile phones while driving. This problem is only going to go up as the number of mobile phone users grow year on year.

Financially, Road crashes cost about USD $518 billion globally, costing individual countries from 1-2% of their annual GDP. Emotionally, the damage it brings to households can be irreparable. Even after hundreds of millions of dollars spent on campaigns to remind public of dangers of speeding and using the mobile phone while driving, speeding and distracted driving is on the rise. History shows that the fear of punishment and respect for the system are two motivators for safe driving, Road Earner aims to introduce the third: Incentivization. By combining Mobile App Technology with Blockchain, driving can be tracked, and drivers can be incentivized for maintaining good track record.

Central idea behind the Road Earner is to encourage safe driving by minimizing the drivers' distractions and rewarding them in return. To create a synergy between drivers and merchants, that will encourage safe driving and will drive business growth.

Ecosystem

Road Earner mobile app will link drivers and merchants together via the Road (ROD) token.

Drivers will use the Road Earner app while driving, that will reduce the outside world communication to bare minimum, such as emergency calls. Successful journeys will be recorded on the blockchain and rewarded in the form of ROD token, that drivers can then use to buy discounted services.

Parents and friends will use ROD tokens to set safe drive targets to young drivers. Upon successful completion of these targets drivers will receive these bonus tokens.

Roard Earner platform will operate on Blockchain technology. Primarily it will server these purposes: Record drivers' driving behaviour and reward ROD tokens, Manage tokens, Manage Marketplace Activities and Manage Users

Merchants will use the Road Earner app to accept ROD tokens from drivers in exchange for discounted goods and services. They then can use these tokens to publish more offers on the Road Earner platform or retain those in their accounts. Thus, making the marketing more effective, greener and saving money.
